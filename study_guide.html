🎯 Key Business Concepts

TAM (Total Addressable Market): The total market demand for a product or service
SaaS (Software as a Service): Software licensing model with recurring revenue
ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue): Predictable annual revenue from subscriptions
Enterprise SaaS: Software solutions designed for large organizations
Zero Trust Security: Security model assuming no implicit trust
Multi-tenancy: Single software instance serving multiple customers

🎯 Red Flags to Avoid

  • ❌ Don't exaggerate metrics
  • ❌ Don't say "no competition"
  • ❌ Don't have unrealistic projections
  • ❌ Don't seem desperate for funding
  • ❌ Don't have unclear use of funds
  • ❌ Don't lack team depth
  • ❌ Don't have weak business model
